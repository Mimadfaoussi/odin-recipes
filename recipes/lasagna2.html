Vegetarian Spinach and Mushroom Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 container (15 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves, chopped
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz) spaghetti sauce
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions; drain.
  2. In a bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, 1 1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and eggs.
  3. In a skillet, cook spinach, mushrooms, and garlic over medium heat until spinach is wilted and mushrooms are tender.
  4. Add spaghetti sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper to the skillet; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a greased 9x13 inch baking dish, layer a third of the sauce, four lasagna noodles, and a third of the cheese mixture; repeat layers twice.
  6. Top with remaining 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese.
  7. Cover and bake at 375Â°F for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake an additional 25 minutes, or until bubbly.
