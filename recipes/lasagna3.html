Seafood Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 lb cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 lb imitation crabmeat, flaked
  • 1 cup chopped fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
  3. Stir in flour, salt, and black pepper until smooth.
  4. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly until mixture comes to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
  5. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted; set aside.
  6. In a large bowl, combine shrimp, crabmeat, spinach, green onions, parsley, and garlic.
  7. In a greased 9x13 inch baking dish, layer four lasagna noodles, a third of the seafood mixture, and a third of the Parmesan sauce. Repeat layers twice.
  8. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  9. Cover and bake at 375Â°F for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake an additional 25 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown on top.
  10. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
